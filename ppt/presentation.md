Some of us turn to Notion AI, Otter, NotebookLM. Great tools — but every single one of them kicks in after class. When you're lost mid-lecture, right now, they're useless.

---

Slide 4

So we asked: what's actually the problem? Distraction isn't it — that's just human. The problem is the recovery. There is no tool built for the moment you snap back and want to rejoin. That gap is what we set out to fill.

---

Slide 5

So we built Peeko — a companion that listens to your lecture with you and brings you back the moment you need it. Not a note-taker. A recovery system.

---

Slide 6

Peeko listens live, generates a summary card every 5 minutes, and stacks them as the lecture runs. Miss 10 minutes? Read 2 cards. You're back — even in the middle of our dreadful 3-hour lectures.

---

Slide 7

And when you're ready to rejoin — one tap. Catch Me Up tells you what's happening right now, what you missed, and exactly what you need to follow along again.

---

Slide 8

The best part? Peeko follows you. Even on YouTube, even on TikTok — our fox floats above every app, quietly showing live keywords from the lecture. A gentle reminder that class is still going. Click it, and you're back.
